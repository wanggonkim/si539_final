Kimchi

A staple Korean side dish made of fermented vegetables with a variety of seasonings.

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Radishes
  • Garlic
  • Chili Flakes
  • Ginger
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Green Onions
  • Water
  • Salted Shrimp
  • Sugar

Bibimbap

A mixed rice dish with an assortment of vegetables, beef, and a fried egg on top.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef or Chicken or Tofu
  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Bean Sprouts
  • Zucchini
  • Egg
  • Gochujang Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Bulgogi

Marinated grilled beef with a perfect balance of sweet and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Green Onions
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Mushrooms
